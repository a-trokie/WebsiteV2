My name is Alex Trokel and I grew up in NYC. I attended Columbia Grammar and Preparatory school from Pre-K through the 12th grade.

After 6th grade, I spent my summers at sleep away camp in Lenox, Massachusetts. Camp was (and still is) a very special place to me. I was able to explore my strengths, develop my leadership skills with my campers and to understand the importance of collaboration with my fellow staff and camp directors. Coming from the perspective of being a camper and then a counselor, I believe that I was able to have a positive impact on my campers and was able to improve the overall camp experience for everyone.

Ever since I was young, I’ve been drawn to the power of computers and technology in general. In high school, I took all of the computer-oriented courses that I could find. I also built my own custom computer! Whether it was Web Design (HTML/CSS) or Computer Math (Mathematica) I took them all, and found myself excelling. This was the breakthrough that sparked my interest in Computer Science.
